Canada Visa:

CANADA TOURIST VISA IF ANY ONE INVITING OR SPONSORING THE VISA

Applicant Document

  • Online application form
  • Two photograph (Size)35x45mm white back ground glossy finish 80% face image
  • Personal covering letter with travel date and expenses mention Note Letter should be address TO, THE VISA OFFICER, CANADA HIGH COMMSSION, DELHI.
  • Pay slip 3 months
  • Personal income tax 3 years
  • Personal bank statement 6 months with bank seal with bank balance of 1, 00,000/-
  • Leave letter from company in company letter head

Inviting Document

  • Invitee passport copy first page and last page and visa copy
  • Invitation letter from Canada
  • Invitee Residence card copy or work permit card copy
  • Invitee bank statement 3 months
  • Invitee pay slips 6 months

IF INVITEE IS SPONSORING THE TRIP DOCUMENTS GIVEN BELOW.
